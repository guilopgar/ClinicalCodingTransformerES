A 44-year-old male was admitted for facial trauma.
The radiographic study revealed a large radiolucent lesion and a displaced angle fracture.
1.
Enucleation of the lesion, extraction of the causing dental roots and reduction-osteosis of the intraosseous wire fracture and mini plate with monocortical injury were performed.
1.
The histological study of the enucleated material revealed an inflammatory radicular cyst.
Bone healing was observed radiographically two months later.

A 16-year-old woman underwent right radical nephrectomy in December 2001 for presenting a renal mass of 8 cm in maximum size, dependent on the lower pole, lobulated contours, and homogeneous density, diagnosed by nonspecific symptoms.
The pathology reported multifocal nephroblastoma of mixed component, blastematose and epithelial.
58 months of systemic chemotherapy with vincristine actin.

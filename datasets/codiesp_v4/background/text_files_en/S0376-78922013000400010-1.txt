A 35-year-old man with mineral oil in the glulites and lumbar region was admitted to hospital in 1995.
He came to our Service for presenting a bloody area ; as a history he reported having undergone partial resection of modeling substance in the lumbar region in 2007 and 2010.
1.
The treatment was based on surgical debridement without aggressive debridement and placement of a subatmospheric pressure system: continuous pressure of 125 mmHg, with which we achieved direct closure of the lesion at 35 days.

A three-year-old male with a one-week history of erythematous, pruritic perianal lesion without fever or malaise was observed.
Radial fissures are also seen in the examination.
A rectal swab was taken with a positive result in the rapid test for Streptococcus. Treatment was initiated with oral amoxicillin and topical mupirocin for ten days, with complete resolution of the symptoms at the end of treatment.
The culture results of the sample confirmed the growth of GABHS.

A 10-year-old girl begins with this condition.
He has left ankle sprain with development of CRPS of ankle and left foot.
She was referred to the Pain Unit by the Pediatric Rheumatology Department four years after diagnosis.
Pharmacological treatment with capsaicin 8 % patch was administered without remission.
Placement in the operating room under general anesthesia and prior paternal consent of a lumbar epidural catheter and connection to an external pump for administration of continuous infusion of bupivacaine for two weeks.
Catheter removal after that time.
Complete remission of symptoms.

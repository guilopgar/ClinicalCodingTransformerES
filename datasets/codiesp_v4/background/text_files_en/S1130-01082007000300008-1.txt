A 15-month-old male with parents who regularly consume salicylates was admitted to our hospital because of stupor, vomiting and diarrhea, with a torpid evolution that led to a deep failure in his admission.
The patient's laboratory test showed an intense elevation of transaminases.
Necropsy revealed marked hepatomegaly with severe steatosis.
The histological study confirmed the intracellular fatty deposit and its microgotular character.

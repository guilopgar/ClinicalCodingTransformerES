A 68-year-old woman presented with a sclerodermiform tumor in the central area of the lower eyelid that freed the appearance of the right eyelid.
Under local anesthesia and sedation, a wide excision of the skin lesion is performed, respecting the internal lamella and reconstruction with myocutaneous flap of the orbicular island muscle with dimensions of pathological anatomy reported basal cell carcinoma.

A 30-year-old healthy female resident in the city of Santiago.
He attended a Japanese food restaurant where he tasted various flavors, some of them prepared with raw salmon.
The next day he began coughing and sneezing, coughing up his mouth with a handkerchief, and he lost the presence of a small mobile white element.
It was taken to a care center, from which the sample was sent to the reference laboratory of Parasitology for identification.
Anisakis sp. larvae were reported.
Subsequently, based on post-diaphanization morphometric studies in Aman lactophenol, Pseudoterranova decipiens was identified.

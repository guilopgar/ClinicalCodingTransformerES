78-year-old male with hypertension, diabetes mellitus, ischemic heart disease, heart failure, peripheral vascular disease, iron deficiency anemia and chronic renal failure.
She continued treatment with furosemide, hydralazine, ferrous sulfate, acetylsalicylic acid, lisinopryl, carvedilol, doxazosine and insulin.
